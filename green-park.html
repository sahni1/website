3. Four Hands Therapy

Enjoy the ultimate in relaxation with our Four Hands Therapy, where two expert therapists collaborate flawlessly to create a symphony of touch and serenity.

4. Balinese Massage

With our Balinese Massage, you may discover the fascinating traditions of Bali. This therapeutic procedure uses acupressure, lengthy strokes, and gentle stretching to bring the body and mind back into harmony.

5. Chocolate Massage

With our delectable Chocolate Massage, indulge your senses in complete happiness. As our knowledgeable therapists apply warm, melting chocolate to your body, take in the rich, wonderful aroma of chocolate. Discover how chocolate can nourish and moisturize your skin, making it silky smooth and incredibly moisturized.

6. Swedish Massage

Our Swedish Massage, a traditional therapy created to encourage relaxation and reduce muscle tension, can help you unwind and rest. To increase blood circulation, lower stress levels, and improve general wellbeing, our therapists use lengthy, gliding strokes, kneading, and circular motions.

7. Touch of Feather massage

Experience the gentle and calming Touch of Feather Massage, a treatment that releases tension and promotes relaxation by using strokes that are as light as a feather. With the help of this mild massage technique, the nerve endings are stimulated, resulting in a profound sense of relaxation and floating.

8. Thai Delight Massage

Get energized by the deep rhythmic movements, stretching, and acupressure used in the energizing Thai Delight Massage. This traditional Thai therapy aims to balance energy flow, reduce muscle tension, and increase flexibility.

9. Essential Aroma Healing ritual

With our Essential Aroma Healing Ritual, you may go on a sensory adventure while also receiving the therapeutic advantages of essential oils and soft massage techniques. Experience the therapeutic benefits of aromatherapy as our therapists customize the treatment to your unique requirements and use essential oils that encourage rest, renewal, or invigoration.

10. Abhayangam Therapy

Experience Abhayangam Therapy, an age-old Ayurvedic healing technique that employs warm herbal oils to massage the entire body and nourish the skin while easing tension in the muscles.

11. Volcanic Rock Therapy

Utilize our volcanic rock therapy to unleash the power of volcanic rocks. Your body is expertly massaged with smooth, hot basalt stones to melt away stress and encourage profound relaxation.

12. Reflexology (foot massage)

Learn about the history of reflexology, a therapy that emphasizes stimulating particular pressure points on the feet to enhance general health.Our therapists use exact pressure techniques on reflex zones, which stand in for various bodily organs and systems.

14. Body Scrub
